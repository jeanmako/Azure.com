Middle School

Develop at your own pace and Get ready for academic and career success.

Grades 6-8

In Elementary School, students start laying the foundation for future success. At this level, they begin to:

  • Develop life skills like critical-thinking and problem-solving

  • Explore their interest and develop an understanding of the world around them

  • Develop a mastery of Basics academic skills

Elementary school is a vital step in preparing children for later learning and life in general. It’s where most children learn to read, where they learn the fundamental concepts of science and mathematics, and where they gain a basic understanding of the world and its history. Without elementary education, all other learning would be difficult, if not impossible. Indeed, studies have shown that learning to read before fourth grade can actually increase a student’s lifelong earning potential. In other words, the quality of education a child receives in elementary school can affect their entire life.

Curriculum

Mathematics

Middle school math comprises topics like Number Systems, Fractions, and Linear Equations which will lay the foundation for advanced math. It also includes topics like Pre-Algebra, Algebra I, and Geometry which will help them with their high school level math.

Science

Students will begin by exploring matter, its physical and chemical properties, and different states. Throughout this middle school science course, students will also learn about the periodic table, chemical reactions, motion, temperature, magnetism, and more.

Language Arts

Middle school language arts focuses on phonics, fluency, grammar, spelling, vocabulary, reading comprehension, writing processes and more.

Social Studies

Social studies for middle schoolers bridges the foundational information they've learned in elementary school with the increasingly complex information they'll encounter later on.

Bible

The lessons will go deeper into the matter of the life of Jesus and the Salvation through Him. The lessons will focus on how He lived, died and rose from the dead, and how we, as followers of Christ ought to live our lives.

Visual Arts

The Middle School Art curriculum encourages personal expression and inventive thinking through the imaginative exploration of art materials and methods. Projects may include but not be limited to: drawing, painting, printmaking, collage, mixed media, pottery, and sculpture.

Foreign Languages

The lessons will help students learn a foreign language of their choice, one level every semester. The languages they will have to choose from are: French, Spanish, German, and Mandarin.

Programming

The lessons be a continuation of the Elementary level class. The programming languages includes are: HTML and CSS (Markup languages), Python, Javascript, C++ and more.

Information technology 101

The lessons will be an introduction to the world of Technology and computing. It will teach students the ethics of technology, the internet, and more.

Artificial intelligence 101

This will be an introduction to the world of AI. How it works, how it's made, what are the pros and cons, the dos and don'ts, and how to make it as a useful tool and not a danger.

The bill of rights 101

An introduction to the worldview shared by the Visionaries Founding Fathers and what values they desired for the United States of America.

Startup 101

An introduction on how to give life to your ideas, and share them with potential investors and/or buyers. You will the the basics of how to build a Pitch Deck and attract buyers and investors.

Entrepreneurship 101

Learn the basics on how to give life to your innovative idea that will make your visions and dreams possible.
