Our mission

Azure exists to provide a safe learning environment for all our students. We encourage students to work hard and to strive to become good citizens and patriots. We also consider it essential to let parents participate and manage their children’s education. We inspire students to love their country and to strive for a better future.

Azure is an online homeschooling platform. We are dedicated to providing an exceptional and transparent learning experience to our students, building trust with parents.
Our vision is to cultivate abundant growth in each and every student, for a bright future.

Why choose us?

Safety

Azure is designed to create a safe learning environment to all our students. We keep our students safe from any type of activism and do our best to help the succeed.

Reliability

The Azure brand has been built on the foundation of trust with parents. We keep our promises made to parents to always let them know about their kid’s education and progress.

Flexibility

Azure is a 100% online school. You can study and review your courses anytime, anywhere, as long as you're connected.

Christianity

Azure believes and shares the Christian values and teachings, and we put God at the center of our students education.

Transparency

We are transparent and open with parents, and we let them decide about their child education.

Affordability

Azure is known for being affordable, considering the different options it includes. The price is equivalent to $2.50/hour of tutoring.

How Azure works:

1

Submit the application form.

Fill out in the Application form in our Apply Now page. Information about the student and parent will be requested.

2

Choose your plan.

You can choose your preferred plan in our Pricing page. You will receive an email from us with the Bank account details to make the payment.

3

Application approval.

We will contact you via email after reviewing your application, and give you your Azure log in credentials.

4

Good to go. Start learning.

Account activated. Start learning with us and get ready to reach your goals.
