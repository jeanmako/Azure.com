Frequently asked questions

How it works

What is Azure ?

Azure is an online homeschooling platform designed to help homeschoolers and parents overcome challenges that come with homeschooling.

How does Azure work ?

As an online platform, Azure offers all its classes and online. All you need is a computer and internet to get started and start learning.

Do you require State testing ?

To make sure you satisfy all the local requirements in your State, we advise you to check with your school district in your state to learn more about the State testing requirements. You can learn about your state requirements here.

How do I apply ?

You can apply by visting the Apply Now page. The application process is easy, can be started online and only takes 15-20 minutes. You will need to enter information about the student and parent, and upload required documents (transcripts(if any), photo, ID).

What are the application steps?

  1. Fill the application form and submit.
  2. Choose and make the payment of your chosen plan.
  3. Receive a confirmation of your application and your Azure credentials(student and parent).
  4. Let the learning begin with Azure.

Academic

Are the offered courses self-paced ?

Yes. Although the courses are live, students do not have to meet any deadline other than the last day of class to submit their work.

How will my student learn at Azure ?

Azure offers all its courses online, which means that all the student needs to get started is a device with an internet connection. The lessons are given live by Azure teachers, and as long as the student is connected to the internet, he can rewatch the lesson anytime and anywhere.

What grades does Azure offer ?

Azure is available to all students in grades PreK-12. You can find more details in these links:

  • Elementary
  • Middle School
  • High School

What courses will my student have?

The course selection is up to the parent. Each semester offers a total of 7 courses that will be chosen and managed by the parents. The student may opt for some non-compulsory courses during a semester.

Will everything for the program be provided online?

Yes. All the lessons and e-books will be provided online.

Will my student be able to participate to extracurricular activities?

Yes. Azure offers some extracurricular activities to its students. The extracurricular activities are optional.

How long does it take to complete a semester?Is it okay if the student go on vacation during the semester?

An academic semester will take 16 weeks to complete. Because Azure is an online platform, students may take a break/vacation. All they would need is an internet connection.

Pricing

What is the Tuition?

The Basic Azure price is $1.499 and the Premium price is $1,649 per semester.

What is covered by the tuition ?

The tuition covers the curriculum and testing fees,tutoring, extracurriculars and sports(optional).

What is the Tuition based on?

The tuition is an estimated tutoring fee of $2.50 per hour, 6 hours a day, for an entire academic semester.

Is ther any discount on the Pricing?

Yes. Parents will get a 15% off on every student of their own if they register 2 students, 30% if they register 3 students, 50% if they register 4 students, and 75% if they register 5 or more.

Student

Where can my student find his Azure credentials?

Upon registration, the student will be assigned a Azure ID and password by email. The ID will be a combination of the student's name and surname so that the ID is not forgotten.

Parent

How do I monitor my student grades and progress?

To monitor all your students grades and process, you have to log into your Azure Parent account, and there you will find all the information about your student'srecord and transcripts.

How do I log into Azure as a Parent?

If your student is enrolled in Azure, you will get a parent account that will help you manage and monitor your student activities. You can login online at the Azure website, or you can download the Azure app on each device that you want, and login as a parent.

Can I enroll students that are not my kids?

It is possible to enroll students that are not your own kids, but you will have to be their legal guardian. Any applicatiom from somebody other than a legal guardian will not be considered.
